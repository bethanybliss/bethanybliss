Straightening natural hair!

There isn't one way to straighten natural hair. Some people can straighten their hair often and others opt for the annual hair straightening ceremony. Whatever you decide, here is one way to make sure you are protecting those tresses the best way you can.

  • Wash your hair and deep condition your hair.
  • Spray your hair with a heat protectant
  • Blow dry your hair on medium to low heat.
  • Flat iron your hair at a temperature of at most 150 degrees Celsius or 302 degree Fahrenheit.
